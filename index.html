ABOUT ME

I have always enjoyed challenges and problem solving, and after attending iO Academy bootcamp I found a real passion for working as part of a team to overcome hurdles and be a productive member of a group.

PORTFOLIO

The Office Quotes Collection

A collection of quotes from The Office - U.S. Sitcom. This project has the ability to add new quotes to a Database which is displayed on the front end using MySQL.
